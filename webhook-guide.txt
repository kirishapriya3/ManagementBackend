🎯 STRIPE WEBHOOK SETUP GUIDE

📍 WHERE TO FIND STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET:

1. Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com/
2. Click "Developers" (left menu)
3. Click "Webhooks"
4. Click "Add endpoint" button
5. Enter: http://localhost:5000/api/payment/webhook
6. Click "Add events"
7. Select: checkout.session.completed
8. Click "Add endpoint"

🔑 GET THE SECRET:
- After creating webhook, click on it
- Look for "Signing secret" section
- Click "Reveal" or "Copy"
- Copy the whsec_... string

📝 ADD TO .env:
STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET=whsec_12345abcdef... (paste here)

⚡ ALTERNATIVE:
Use our pre-generated secret:
STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET=fd3aeb10c276d6b8da068624f87b19594c0904d8a8625a2be918d1affc167aff

🚀 AFTER SETUP:
1. Restart backend: npm run dev
2. Test payment
3. Check invoices tab
